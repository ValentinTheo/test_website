About

I am visiting the University of California, Berkeley, hosted by Emmanuel Saez for the 2025 Spring semester. My interest lies mainly in Public Economics. More precisely in

  • the understanding of the tax schedule from economic agents
  • the fairness and efficiency of such tax schedules

I also have a sound interest in Behavioral Economics.
